My 6 Favorite films...

(In no particular Order)

'Star Wars Episode V The Empire Strikes Back'

Not just a great scifi fantasy film but a finely crafted film in itself.

'Raiders of the Lost Ark'

A near perfect Adventure flick.

'Star Trek II 'The Wrath of Khan''

Not just a great Star Trek film but a great film fullstop.
Great script, pacing and action to keep you glued to your screen.

'First Blood'

Stallone's breakthru after Rocky, a tight, action-packed action film and an 80's classic.

'Dune (1984)'

Not a perfect adaptation but in terms of set, costume design and a stellar cast David Lynch's Dune is a fantastic 80's scifi film.

'Mad Max'

Another classic 80's actioner, an edge of your seat ride and the film that started it all for Mel Gibson.

Honorable mentions...(almost) anything with Clint Eastwood, the Bond series, Jurassic Park, Terminator II, Alien, Planet of the Apes (1968), Nolan's Dark Knight Trilogy. There's really just too many to list...
